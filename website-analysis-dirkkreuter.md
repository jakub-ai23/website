## 6. CONVERSION STRATEGIES USED

1. **Countdown timers** on events and the hero section
2. **No pricing shown** for coaching/mentoring — forces consultation call (qualifies leads)
3. **Case studies with specific numbers** (983% growth, 3,233% growth)
4. **Multiple CTAs per page** — never more than 1-2 scroll lengths without a button
5. **Scarcity signals** — "250 Tickets available," limited hotel contingent
6. **Newsletter incentive** — 10% shop discount for subscribing
7. **Video testimonials** alongside written case studies
8. **Industry filtering** on case studies so visitors find someone "like them"
9. **Consultation-first model** — all high-ticket products route to a call, not checkout
10. **GDPR-compliant forms** with clear opt-in language

---

## 7. KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR YOUR SITE

1. **The dark + accent color premium aesthetic works** — it signals high-ticket, serious, professional
2. **Video background hero + lead capture pop-up** is the centerpiece — adapt this directly
3. **Transformation section (before/after)** is essential for your positioning
4. **Build a product ladder** — don't just offer one thing
5. **Never show high-ticket prices publicly** — route to a consultation
6. **Case studies are king** — even 3-5 strong ones with real numbers beat generic testimonials
7. **The site is built on Next.js** — modern, fast, SEO-friendly framework
8. **Every section has a purpose** in the conversion funnel — nothing is decorative
9. **Press page adds credibility** — even a simple one with professional photos and bio helps
10. **Filterable success stories** let every visitor find someone like them
